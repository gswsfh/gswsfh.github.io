I am Fu Hao, a Ph.D. candidate in Cybersecurity at Computer Network Information Center(CNIC), Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Sun Degang. My research focuses on baseline construction for network intrusion detection, traffic behavior modeling, and adaptive security defense, with the goal of building robust, interpretable, and label-free anomaly detection baselines in dynamic, heterogeneous, and privacy-constrained network environments.

I embrace the philosophy of "Research as a Service": all my research outputs—including papers, source code, datasets, and pre-trained models—are openly released via https://github.com/gswsfh to promote reproducible research and community collaboration in cybersecurity.
